About Me

✨ ✨

Hello! I'm a passionate student designer and front-end developer currently studying at Eastern Washington University. I love crafting digital experiences that feel both beautiful and intuitive — blending visual design with clean, semantic code.

My design philosophy is inspired by the charm of boutique cafes, the clarity of Swiss typography, and the warmth of handcrafted details. I believe great design should be accessible, responsive, and delightful to interact with.

When I'm not coding or designing, you can find me collecting vintage typography books, exploring local coffee shops, and sketching UI ideas in my notebook. I'm always looking for new ways to merge creativity with technology.
