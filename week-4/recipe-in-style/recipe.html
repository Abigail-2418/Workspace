Soft Cinnamon Rolls

Introduction

These cinnamon rolls are warm, fluffy, and filled with a sweet cinnamon-sugar swirl. They’re perfect for breakfast, brunch, or a cozy dessert.

About This Recipe

This recipe creates extra soft cinnamon rolls with a rich brown sugar filling and creamy icing. The dough is slightly sweet and best enjoyed fresh out of the oven.
For best results, let the dough rise fully so the rolls become light and fluffy.

Ingredients

  • 3 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm milk
  • ⅓ cup sugar

Instructions

  1. Mix warm milk, yeast, and sugar, then let it sit until foamy.
  2. Add flour and knead the dough until smooth and elastic.
  3. Roll out dough, spread butter, cinnamon, and brown sugar, then roll it up and slice.
  4. Bake at 350°F for 20–25 minutes until golden brown.

Notes

Tip: For extra gooey rolls, drizzle icing while they are still slightly warm.
